  • The Flesh-Network is my more or less personal page. It is not necessarily designed to hold any form of readable content, and rather serves as a repository for my own, and if circumstance has it, other people's writings and thoughts. You may stick around, though, if you so desire.

Please do not laugh at them. - Please do talk about them! Thank you.
